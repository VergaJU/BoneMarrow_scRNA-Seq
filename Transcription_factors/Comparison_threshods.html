Conclusion¶

Not sure about the conclusion, is evident that more than half of the TF that reponds to ICRs found with the strict threshold is found with the loose threshold. Also, many interesting ICR are shared by the two datasets. With the ICR regulators the story is different but it could derive by the fact that we still have to expand that list of genes. Since using the stricter threshold I lost most of the cells, I think I will continue working with the previous data.

Curiosity: Mediators/regulators¶

Some ICR mediators works as well as ICR regulators. These TFs regulates both ICR and their response, probably these TFs would be interesting hubs. Also, the presence of TF that receive signal from ICRs and regulates them suggest the presence of loops in the network.
